<h3>Check spring #32758 original but slightly bent out of shape, still okay, 
but a really damaged spring will need replacing</h3>
<h3>Note the position of the spring</h3>
<div align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.08.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></div>
<h3>If the tail of the #32575 check spring curls around on itself like this, 
it is a good idea to trim about 5mm off with a pair of side cutters</h3>
<h3>Note the position of the spring</h3>
<div align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.07.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></div>
<p align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.07a.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></p>
<p align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.S3.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></p>
<h3 align="left">The check spring regulator may not sit flush with the 
end of the tension stud if you don't trim the #32575 check spring, and 
this may affect the effectiveness of the tension release pin (the 25mm 
long rod which runs up the centre of the tension stud).</h3>
<div align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.09.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></div>
<h3>This is how you want it to look, check the spring moves freely</h3>
<h3>Retighten the cap screw when it looks correct</h3>
<div align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.10.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></div>
<h3>The top of the regulator slot should sit about even with the end of 
the minus sign on the tension indicator and the arm of the spring should 
rest against the top of the slot</h3>
<div align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.11.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></div>
<h3>Replace the tension with the presser foot in the down position (tension 
release inactivated) and ensure the centre mark is at 12 o'clock</h3>
<div align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.12.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></div>
<div align="right"><img class="img-fluid" src="{{ "pic/TEN-99K31.13.jpg" | relative_url }}" width="600" height="480"></div>
<h3>Tighten the set screw to the side of the tension and check the tension 
is released when the presser foot is raised</h3>
